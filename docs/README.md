# PDEV Workflow Documentation

Automated workflows powering the PDEV (Process & Development Engineering) operation at Scale Virtually.

| # | Workflow | What It Does |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | [Automated Error Monitoring & Bug Reporting](pdev-error-trigger.md) | When a workflow breaks, the team knows instantly — with a ticket already created. |
| 2 | [Auto-Create Client Requirements](at-create-pdev-requirement.md) | New staffing requirements appear in the internal portal the moment they're logged. |
| 3 | [Auto-Create Point of Contact (POC) Records](at-create-poc.md) | New contacts are added once and shared across all systems instantly. |
| 4 | [Automated RFP Processing Pipeline](at-new-rfp-form-submission.md) | A single form submission triggers the creation of up to 20+ records across three systems — in seconds. |
| 5 | [Master Integration — Three Systems in Sync](at-pdev-master-integration.md) | Airtable, Bubble, and ClickUp stay perfectly aligned without anyone touching them. |
| 6 | [Client Data Reverse Sync (Bubble → Airtable)](bubble-bubble-to-at-sync-clients.md) | Changes made in the internal portal flow back to internal records automatically. |
| 7 | [Project PE Assignment Sync](bubble-to-at-sync-projects.md) | PE assignments in Bubble sync to Airtable project and requirement records automatically. |
| 8 | [Daily PDEV Health Digest & Metrics](daily-pdev-health-metrics.md) | Every requirement and project gets a daily health snapshot, trend history, and a prioritized Slack digest — automatically. |
| 9 | [Billable Activities Tracker](scheduled-billable-activities.md) | Time logs fetched, cross-referenced, and turned into billable activity records — no manual reconciliation. |
| 10 | [AI-Powered Meeting Minutes (MoM) Generator](fathom-pec-mom-generator.md) | Every client meeting is automatically transcribed, summarized, and distributed — no one has to take notes. |
| 11 | [Automated MoM Email Processing](gmail-mom-fetcher.md) | Incoming MoM emails are read by AI, matched to the right project, and logged automatically. |
| 12 | [AI-Powered Client Insight Extraction](gmail-insights-fetcher.md) | Every client email is scanned for project insights and logged automatically — nothing falls through the cracks. |
| 13 | [Daily AI Insights Report](scheduled-pdev-insights.md) | An AI reads all the day's insights, writes a narrative summary, and posts a visual report card to Slack. |
