AI Reply Classification

ActiveAI Engine12 steps

When a prospect replies to an outreach message, the outreach platform captures the reply and forwards it to the system. The AI reads the response and classifies the intent — interested, not interested, out of office, or requesting more information. The classification is logged to the tracking sheets and the team is notified with the result. No reply goes unclassified.

Before

  • Replies read and classified manually by team members
  • Inconsistent classification — different people categorized differently
  • Some replies missed or delayed, especially during high-volume campaigns
  • No centralized record of classification decisions

After

  • Every reply classified automatically by AI — consistent categories every time
  • Classification logged to tracking sheets instantly
  • Team notified with the result in real time
  • 100% capture rate — no reply goes unclassified

Impact

  • Time saved: ~3-5 min per reply — no manual reading and categorizing
  • Cognitive load reduced: No inconsistent classifications, no missed replies, no split attention monitoring inboxes

Lead capture

Meeting Booking Tracker

Active27 steps (3 workflows)

When a prospect books a meeting through the scheduling platform, the system captures the booking instantly, validates the lead against existing warm-lead and previously-contacted lists, and logs it to the tracking sheets. A real-time notification is sent to the team the moment a booking comes in. Each client campaign has its own dedicated tracker.

Before

  • Bookings manually checked and logged from the scheduling platform
  • No automated validation against warm leads or previously contacted lists
  • Data scattered across sheets with inconsistent formatting
  • Team notified informally, sometimes hours after a booking

After

  • Every booking captured, validated, and logged instantly
  • Each client campaign tracked separately with consistent format
  • Real-time team notification the moment a booking comes in

Impact

  • Time saved: ~5-10 min per booking — no manual logging or cross-referencing
  • Cognitive load reduced: Zero missed bookings, no manual dedup, consistent tracking across all clients

Campaign reporting

3

Daily Campaign Reporting

Active14 steps

Every morning, the system connects to the outreach platform, fetches campaign statistics for all active clients, aggregates the data per client, and updates the daily stats sheet. A summary notification is posted to the team — ready for review before the day starts.

Before

  • Campaign stats pulled manually from the outreach platform for each client
  • Data aggregated by hand into spreadsheets
  • Reporting inconsistent — some days it didn't happen at all
  • No centralized view of multi-client campaign performance

After

  • Stats fetched automatically every morning for all active clients
  • Data aggregated per client and upserted into the daily stats sheet
  • Summary notification posted automatically — consistent daily cadence
  • Multi-client performance visible before the day starts

Impact

  • Time saved: ~15-20 min daily — no manual platform login or copy-paste
  • Cognitive load reduced: Consistent morning reporting without effort — no risk of missed days

System operations

4

System Operations

ActiveIntegration16 steps (3 workflows)

When a client's campaign status changes in the tracking sheet, the system automatically activates or deactivates the corresponding booking tracker workflow and notifies the team. Webhook registrations for new clients are handled through a setup utility. When any workflow encounters an error, a ticket is created, the issue is logged for analysis, and the team is alerted within seconds.

Before

  • Workflows manually activated or deactivated when client campaigns changed status
  • Webhook registrations set up by hand for every new client
  • Errors discovered hours or days later, with manual investigation
  • No centralized history of what went wrong and when

After

  • Campaign status changes automatically activate/deactivate the right workflows
  • Webhook registration automated through a setup utility
  • Errors caught instantly with automatic ticket creation and logging
  • Campaign on/off state always matches the source of truth

Impact

  • Cognitive load reduced: No manual workflow management, no missed errors, no stale campaign states
  • Consistency: Campaign activation always matches the tracking sheet — single source of truth
