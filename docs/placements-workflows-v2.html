VA lifecycle management

1

VA Data Management

Active44 steps (8 workflows)

When a VA's status changes — endorsed, placement-ready, entering limbo, or fast-tracked — the system reacts immediately. Endorsement emails are built and sent with consistent formatting, placement-ready VAs get a two-touch notification sequence, stalled candidates are flagged daily before they slip through the cracks, and STP candidates get dedicated communication channels with automatic notifications at every stage.

Before

  • Endorsement emails written by hand with inconsistent formatting
  • VAs not always told promptly when they reached placement-ready status
  • Stalled candidates discovered during manual reviews, sometimes weeks late
  • STP communication channels created manually with inconsistent naming
  • Coordinators had to remember every follow-up for every VA

After

  • Endorsement emails built and sent automatically, formatted consistently every time
  • Two-touch notification sequence for every placement-ready VA
  • Stalled candidates flagged daily — no one slips through the cracks
  • STP channels created automatically with consistent naming
  • Every status change triggers the right action, every time

Impact

  • Time saved: ~35 min per VA lifecycle event — no manual email drafting, no manual channel creation
  • Cognitive load reduced: No forgotten follow-ups, no missed status changes, consistent process for every VA regardless of who's on shift
2

Pipeline Management

ActiveIntegration59 steps (4 workflows)

From interview scheduling through onboarding and CRM sync — the full placement pipeline runs automatically. Calendar events are created with correct timezones and attendees, the complete onboarding email suite fires in sequence (logistics to client, endorsement to operations, congratulations to VA), a dedicated support channel is created, and external CRM records stay in sync in real time.

Before

  • Calendar events created by hand with risk of wrong times or missing attendees
  • Three separate onboarding messages sent manually — logistics, internal handoff, welcome note
  • Support channels created and welcome messages written every time
  • CRM records entered twice — once in the main system, once externally
  • Total: 30–45 min per placement

After

  • Calendar events created automatically — no timezone mistakes, no missed attendees
  • One trigger sends the entire onboarding sequence automatically
  • Support channel created and welcome message posted instantly
  • External CRM synced in real time with success/failure notifications
  • Seconds, zero manual coordination

Impact

  • Time saved: 30–45 min → seconds per placement — no manual scheduling, no dual-entry
  • Cognitive load reduced: Zero scheduling errors, no missed onboarding steps, both systems always in sync
3

Readiness & Reporting

Active46 steps (3 workflows)

Every week, VAs receive automated check-ins about their availability and readiness for placement, and their responses are collected centrally. STP pipeline metrics are computed on daily and weekly schedules, with visual report cards delivered to stakeholders automatically. When a VA is cancelled, the team is notified instantly — no delays.

Before

  • No structured way to check in with VAs about their readiness
  • No daily or weekly reporting existed for STP candidates
  • Cancellations communicated informally — the team sometimes found out days later
  • Getting a pipeline update meant someone stopping to compile one by hand

After

  • VAs get a weekly automated check-in — responses collected centrally
  • Visual report cards generated on daily and weekly cadences automatically
  • Cancellations trigger instant team notifications — zero delay
  • Consistent reporting cadence — no one has to remember to ask

Impact

  • New capabilities: STP reporting and structured readiness tracking didn't exist before
  • Cognitive load reduced: Consistent weekly check-in and reporting cadence without manual outreach or follow-up

4

AI Insights & Daily Report

ActiveAI Engine27 steps (2 workflows)

Every client reply to a candidate shortlist is read by the AI — it understands the intent (accept, reject, or request for more info) and updates the candidate record automatically. No reply goes unnoticed. At the end of each shift, the AI reviews all active placement records, writes an executive summary of the day's activity — wins, risks, and pipeline health — and delivers a visual report card to stakeholders.

Before

  • Coordinators manually checked for client replies throughout the day
  • Every reply had to be read, interpreted, and typed into the candidate record by hand
  • Replies sometimes missed or delayed, leaving candidates waiting
  • No daily placement summary existed — getting an update meant someone stopping to compile one

After

  • Every client reply captured and processed automatically — intent understood instantly
  • Candidate records update themselves — no typing required
  • Executive summary written automatically at the end of every shift
  • 100% capture rate on replies + net-new daily reporting capability

Impact

  • Time saved: ~10 min per reply + net-new end-of-shift reporting
  • Cognitive load reduced: No split attention monitoring inboxes, no candidates left in limbo, consistent end-of-shift visibility for leadership

Daily operations & monitoring

5

Daily Placements Health Digest

Active30 steps (2 workflows)

Health metrics are computed daily for every active placement — scored objectively by urgency, not subjective review. A prioritized digest is posted to the team every morning, surfacing the placements that need attention first.

Before

  • Someone manually reviewed every active placement each morning
  • Urgency assessed subjectively — inconsistent from person to person
  • Some days the review didn't happen at all, and issues slipped through

After

  • Health scores are calculated automatically every day for every active placement
  • A prioritized summary is shared with the team every morning
  • The digest happens every day — no exceptions

Impact

  • Time saved: ~20-30 min daily
  • Cognitive load reduced: Objective scoring removes guesswork — consistent visibility, no missed mornings

After

Impact
